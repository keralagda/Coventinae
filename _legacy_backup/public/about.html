About Us

Benevure Craft Pvt Ltd is into hospatility service, it has started one of the largest Luxary Houseboat in Kerala, which Cruise at Kumarakom and Aleppy the word “Benevure” was coined by our founder Rohan Michael Sebastine taking two separate words, benevolent and future as a portmanteau. The boat offers luxury, peace and comfort from the hectic rigors of everyday life as also joy and happiness from parties, marriages etc. It also has an artistically designed board room for corporate meetings. It mainly offer corporate events and conference on the cruise.

About Us

Coventina Houseboat – The Best Houseboat in Kerala

Welcome aboard Coventina Houseboat, your gateway to the serene backwaters of Kerala. Renowned as the best houseboat in Kerala, we offer a perfect blend of traditional charm and modern luxury to give you an unforgettable cruising experience.

  • Unmatched Houseboat Experience:
    At Coventina Houseboat, we believe every journey should be as beautiful as the destination. Our houseboats are designed to capture the soul of Kerala with elegant interiors, delicious local cuisine, and the soothing rhythm of the backwaters. Whether you’re planning a romantic getaway, a family trip, or a relaxing escape, we ensure every moment feels special.
  • Warm Hospitality:
    Our dedicated crew takes care of every detail — from safety and comfort to hospitality and authentic local experiences. As you drift along the tranquil waters surrounded by lush greenery and village life, you’ll discover why Coventina is celebrated as the best houseboat in Kerala.
  • A Timeless Experience:
    Enjoy the breathtaking Alleppey backwaters where luxury meets tradition and nature welcomes you with open arms.

Food and Cuisine

And when it comes to food, we take you on a culinary voyage across India — from the rich spices of the North to the tangy delights of the South, the bold flavors of the East to the savory tastes of the West. Every meal is a celebration of India’s diverse cuisine, freshly prepared onboard by our expert chefs.
